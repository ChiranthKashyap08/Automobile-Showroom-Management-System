BMW R18

BMW R18 is a cruiser bike available at a starting price of Rs. 19,89,819 in India. It is available in 3 variants and 3 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 24,00,000. The BMW R18 is powered by 1802cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 89.84 bhp and a torque of 158 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, BMW R18 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This R18 bike weighs 345 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 16 liters.
