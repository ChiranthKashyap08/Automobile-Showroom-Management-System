Kawasaki Ninja 650

Kawasaki Ninja 650 is powered by 649 cc engine. This Ninja 650 engine generates a power of 68 PS @ 8000 rpm and a torque of 64 Nm @ 6700 rpm. Kawasaki Ninja 650 gets Disc brakes in the front and rear. The kerb weight of Ninja 650 is 196 Kg.

Kawasaki Z1000

Z1000 is powered by a 1,043cc inline-four liquid-cooled engine that churns out 142bhp at 10,000rpm and a torque of 111Nm at 7,300 rpm. The motorcycle makers have revised the ECU settings, made changes to the camshaft timings, fitted a new air box and have connected the cylinders to reduce pumping losses.
